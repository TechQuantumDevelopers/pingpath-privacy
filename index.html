Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

PingPath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data in compliance with the Google Play Developer Program Policies.

Prominent Disclosure: PingPath collects location data to enable geographic alarms to wake you up when approaching your destination, even when the app is closed or not in use.

1. Information Collection and Use

Precise Background Location Data

To provide its core proximity alert service, PingPath accesses and processes your device's precise location coordinates (latitude and longitude). This data is collected and processed in the background, even when the app is closed or not actively in use, to calculate the remaining distance to your target destination and trigger the alarm precisely on time.

Local Storage and Media Files

PingPath accesses your local audio and media storage solely to allow you to select and play custom alarm ringtones from your device's files. We do not access other media, photos, or documents, and we do not scan your storage for unrelated purposes.

2. Data Processing and Transmission

Data Transmission / Sharing Guarantee: All location tracking and audio file processing happens entirely locally on your device. Your location coordinates and custom audio data never leave the device, are never stored on external servers, and are never shared, transmitted, or sold to any third parties.

3. Local Storage and Data Retention

  • Local Location Processing: Real-time location data is calculated dynamically in memory on your device and is discarded immediately after processing. It is never saved to a permanent travel history.
  • Saved Destinations: Coordinates and names for your custom saved or recent alerts are stored securely inside a local database on your device. This data is fully under your control.
  • User Deletion: You can completely clear your search history and saved alerts at any time directly through the app settings or recent logs.

4. Device Information and Background Execution

We may read your device manufacturer and model name to provide device-specific guides for battery optimization. This allows PingPath to run reliably in the background without being unexpectedly closed by the Android system's aggressive battery saving policies.

5. Security

We implement standard, secure local data storage models to protect all settings and saved alert data on your device. Since your data is not transmitted to external servers, its security also depends on your device's security configurations (such as screen locks and hardware-level encryption).

6. Children's Privacy

PingPath is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since no data is collected off-device, we do not compile or store profiles of any users, including children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any changes.
